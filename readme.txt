Best Posts Widgets shows random posts from your blog in your sidebar, particularly handy for showing
the best posts on your blog. 

== Installation ==

1. Upload `best-posts-widget` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`
1. Active the Widget via the Plugins Tab
1. Use the Presentation - Widgets and then configure to your requirements


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

* Are there any requirements?

Yes - GD running on your server. Check www.php.net/gd for information
